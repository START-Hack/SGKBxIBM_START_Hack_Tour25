Clanky - More money in your pocket, withour changing your lifestyle

Clanky is a personalized AI finance expert in your pocket.
It analyzes your transactions to detect hidden costs, forgotten subscriptions, and smarter partner offers turning complex financial data into fun, easy-to-digest insights.
Unlike budgeting apps that push you to spend less, Clanky helps you save more while living the same life, ensuring you end each month with more left over.
